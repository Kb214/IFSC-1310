Progressive Enhancement Thoughts

I think that Progressive Enhancement is a very great way to build a website. I like how we have steps to take on what to focus on rather than how graceful degradation focuses on the web browser capabilities.To me Progressive Enhancement basically just gives us a basic groundwork on website building with content being the end all be all. Without the content why is the user there in the first place if we don’t have anything and it’s not cohesive? I think that Graceful Degradation could be useful when the website is already finished, you’ve already worked on the important stuff that makes a website and now all you have to do with Graceful Degradation is to make it better on popular search engines.
